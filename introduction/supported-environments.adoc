== {PRODUCT_SHORT} {VERSION_910} Decision Services Support

Decision services are supported by IBM, provided they run in production on an IBM OpenShift Container Platform version in Full or Maintenance support in the Stable and EUS channels of OpenShift.

Any fixes, patches, and CVEs for Kogito in BAMOE are provided only until the next BAMOE release.

Kogito decision services are supported at least for the length of BAMOE 9.1 support.
